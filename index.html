Ask A Question?

Have any doubts, if not yet satisfied feel free to chat with the IZITA team.

Which are the dates of IZITA 2.0?
IZITA 2.0 is a Four day National event hosted by IEEE SB ASIET, starting from 16th of August 2018 and ending on 19th of August 2018.
How can I register for the event?
There are several register buttons embedded. You can use any of the links to make your payment and register your seats for the event.
Do I have to pay for the Accommodation and Food?
The payments for accoommodation and food are also included in the registration amount collected and no additional payments are required.
What kind of payments do you accept?
Debit cards, Credit cards, Netbanking, E- Wallets and UPI transactions can be done through the payment portal.
What all I have to carry to the event?
Every delegate should bring the valid proof of payment and Identity card issued by your respective Institution. Also you may have to bring in Laptops for the respective workshop or session.
How can I confirm my registration?
For any quereies regarding the online registration please contact Arjun A (8848289378) or drop a mail to arjunangepat@gmail.com.
I need help to clarify my doubts on the workshops and sessions?
Feel free to use our online chat support, Our volunteers are happy to help you round the clock anytime regarding any concerns.
How much does the registration cost?
All the necessary details regarding the payments are shown in the payment portal. Refer it for more information.
